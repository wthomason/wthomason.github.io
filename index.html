About William

I'm a dedicated husband and father to my wife and four amazing children. I love to spend time with my family camping, fishing, hiking and hunting. Besides web development one of my many passions is Bow hunting for whitetail deer. I have served in the Iowa Army National guard as Chinook helicopter mechanic for 15 years with two deployments to Kuwait and Iraq.

Amongst my many passions in life one of the biggest is web development. I love learning newest technologies and implementing them in my projects I began my journey in web development in 2015 and attended Des Moines Area Community College (DMACC) and received my Associates in Applied Science Degree majoring in Web Development. I also took courses in graphic design, photography and digital marketing. Since graduating from DMACC I have worked as a Contract web developer and at Mail Services LLC. I have been working towards my Bachelors Degree in Computer Science at Bellevue University.
